What a client sees — and doesn't see — in Sofia's intro

Unclear services
Does she do standard cleans? Deep cleans? Move-outs? Post-construction? The intro doesn't say — the client has to guess from the pills below.
Weak credibility

Missing differentiator

The four warning signals you're looking for

When reviewing a pro's profile intro, these are the patterns that quietly suppress conversion:

  • Generic language — phrases any pro in any category could use
  • Unclear services — the client can't tell what the pro actually does
  • Weak credibility — no years, reviews, certifications, or results in the intro
  • Placeholder descriptions — text that reads like a default template that was never updated

Sofia's profile has warmth — that's worth preserving. What it's missing is specifics. A client reading this intro has no reason to choose Sofia over the next cleaner in the list. That's the gap you'll learn to close.
